Chicken-alfredo

Ingridients

  • 1 LB Uncooked Fettuccine
  • 2 Cubed Chicken Breast Halves
  • 1 (16 ounce) Jar Alfredo-Style Pasta Sauce
  • 1 (10 ounces) package frozen mixed veggies
  • 1 (4.5 ounces) can sliced shroomies
  • 1/3 cup whole milk

Directions

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water. Cook fettuccine at boil until Al-Dente and drain
  2. Combine chicken, alfredo sauce, veggies shroomies and milk in large saucepan over medium. Cook and stir until chicken is heated through and veggies are tender
  3. Serve warm alfredo and chicken sauce over cooked noodles
